about me:

im a 15-year-old from bogotá, colombia who loves writing and making music, coding, and learning.
im a native spanish speaker, but i also have a C1 level in english and an A2 level in french. im trying to learn japanese!
ive made music for as long as i can remember. ive made arrangements for pieces such as "luminaire" and "forward" by carlos eiene, "juana jacinta" by victoriano valencia, and "sir duke" by stevie wonder. im also working on a bambuco for concert band that will be presented on the "concurso nacional del bambuco inédito" in tocancipá.
i joined hack club in late 2024, and have so far participated in various events such as hackpad, high seas, hackapet, the athena award, and more.
